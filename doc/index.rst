A sea ice plugin for Veros
==========================

Veris adds a sea ice sub-model on top of the `Veros <https://veros.readthedocs.io/en/latest>`_ ocean model.

Veris is based on `SEAICE package <https://mitgcm.readthedocs.io/en/latest/phys_pkgs/seaice.html>`_ by `Martin Losch et al. <https://www.awi.de/ueber-uns/organisation/mitarbeiter/detailseite/martin-losch.html>`_, Alfred Wegener institute.
